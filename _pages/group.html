Call for PhD Students

I am actively looking for motivated PhD students to join my research group at the University of Manchester. I particularly welcome applicants with strong backgrounds in control theory, optimisation, reinforcement learning, or robotics who are enthusiastic about developing advanced control and decision-making methods for complex autonomous systems.

Funding opportunities, such as President’s Doctoral Scholar (PDS) and China Scholarship Council (CSC) awards, are available from the University. For more details about these and other funding schemes, please visit the Faculty of Science and Engineering PhD Funding Page. If you already have your own funding to support your PhD studies, please make sure to highlight this information clearly when you first contact me at zhongguo.li@manchester.ac.uk.
